# Commercial License for Classly

## Overview

Classly is available under a **dual-licensing model**:

1. **Community License** (free) – For personal, educational, and non-profit use
2. **Commercial License** (paid) – For businesses, commercial use, and SaaS deployments

This document describes the Commercial License option for organizations that require
rights beyond what the Community License permits.

## When Do You Need a Commercial License?

You need a Commercial License if any of the following applies:

| Use Case | Commercial License Required? |

| Use in schools, universities, or educational institutions | No |
| Use by registered non-profit organizations | No |
| Use within a for-profit company (any size) | **Yes** |
| Internal business tools or operations | **Yes** |

| Integrating into commercial products | **Yes** |

## Commercial License Benefits

A Commercial License grants you the following rights:

- **Unlimited Commercial Use:** Deploy and use Classly within your organization
  without restrictions.

- **SaaS Rights:** Offer Classly as a hosted service to your customers.

- **Multi-Tenant Deployments:** Operate Classly for multiple clients or organizations.

- **White-Label Rights:** Rebrand and customize Classly for your product offerings
  (depending on license tier).

- **Priority Support:** Access to dedicated support channels and faster response times.

- **Warranty and Indemnification:** Legal protections not available under the
  Community License (depending on license tier).

- **Influence on Roadmap:** Enterprise customers may have input on feature
  prioritization and development.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I try Classly before purchasing a Commercial License?

Yes. You may evaluate Classly for a reasonable evaluation period (typically 30 days)
to determine if it meets your needs. Production use by for-profit organizations
requires a Commercial License.

### Do I need a Commercial License for internal tools?

Yes. Any use within a for-profit organization, including internal tools and
business operations, requires a Commercial License.

### What if I'm a freelancer or consultant?

If you are using Classly for client projects or as part of billable work, you
need a Commercial License. Personal learning and development do not require one.

### Can I get a discount for early-stage startups?

Yes. We offer special pricing for early-stage startups. Contact us to discuss
your situation.

### What happens if I used Classly under the old MIT License?

Versions released under the MIT License (commit d29a12d and earlier) remain
under the MIT License for those versions. The new licensing applies only to
versions released after the license change.
